# Design Context: ShopHub

## Users
- **Target Audience**: Shop Owners (Admins), Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Staff, Sales Staff.
- **Context**: High-pressure environment (100-500 orders/day) requiring quick, accurate decisions.
- **Jobs to be Done**: Monitor business health, manage multi-platform inventory/orders, analyze performance trends.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Professional, Trustworthy, Efficient.
- **3-Word Personality**: **Clarity**, **Action-Oriented**, **Calm**.
- **Emotional Goals**: Confidence in data, feeling of control over complex operations.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Clean B2B SaaS, Refined Minimalism.
- **Theme**: Light Mode (primary), Dark Mode (for night shifts).
- **Colors**: Indigo Blue (Primary), Slate (Neutral), Coral Orange (Accent/CTA).
- **Typography**: Be Vietnam Pro (Clean, Vietnamese-friendly), JetBrains Mono (Precise numeric data).
- **References**: Linear, Stripe, Modern Dashboard designs.

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity First**: Data must be unambiguous. Use `[color] + [icon] + [label]` for status clarity.
2. **Progressive Disclosure**: Hide complexity behind hover/click. Use skeletons for loading.
3. **Consistent Density**: Maintain spacing (8px grid) to prevent visual overwhelm.
4. **Action-Oriented**: Every view should suggest the next step or insight.
5. **AI Transparency**: Clearly mark AI-generated insights with confidence scores and "Sparkles" icons.
